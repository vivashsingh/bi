About Bula Impact

We are storytellers, designers, and technologists based in Suva, Fiji. Bula Impact was founded in 2020 with a simple belief: the Pacific deserves world‑class digital craftsmanship – and so do our clients everywhere. Our name, “Bula”, is a Fijian greeting that means life, health, and warmth. It reflects how we work: with genuine care, deep listening, and a commitment to bringing your brand’s story to life.

But we don’t force a “Fijian look” on anyone. Every brand has its own identity, and our job is to uncover it. Whether you’re a local Fijian business or a global enterprise, we adapt our design approach to your unique culture, audience, and goals. Our Pacific roots give us a perspective that values authenticity, community, and connection – values that resonate worldwide.

Our mission: To help brands discover what makes them unique and express it through digital experiences that are beautiful, functional, and human.

Our team: 100% Fijian‑owned, with collaborators around the globe. We’re small, agile, and deeply passionate about our craft.

Location: Based in Suva, Fiji, we work with clients in the Pacific, New Zealand, Australia, North America, and beyond.
